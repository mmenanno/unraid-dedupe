Cron Expression Help

A cron expression consists of 5 fields:

minute hour day month day_of_week
  • minute: 0-59
  • hour: 0-23
  • day: 1-31
  • month: 1-12
  • day_of_week: 0-6 (0=Sunday)

Special characters:

  • * - any value
  • */n - every n units
  • n-m - range from n to m
  • n,m - values n and m
