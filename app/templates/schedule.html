Cron Expression Help

A cron expression consists of 5 fields:

minute hour day month day_of_week

Field Values

minute: 0-59
hour: 0-23
day: 1-31
month: 1-12
day_of_week: 0-6 (0=Sunday)

Special Characters

* any value
*/n every n units
n-m range from n to m
n,m values n and m

Example: 0 2 * * 0 = Every Sunday at 2:00 AM
